## Tips for Facebook Success

1. **Attach the video** - way more engagement than text
2. **Use emojis** - Facebook loves 'em
3. **Ask a question** - drives comments ("What other lakes?")
4. **Reply to comments fast** - boosts visibility
5. **Join group first** - Participate for a day before posting
6. **Be helpful** - Answer questions in comments
